# Big Heart Pet Brands (big-heart-pet-brands)
Big Heart Pet Brands was a producer and marketer of branded pet food and snacks with brands including Meow Mix, Milk-Bone, Kibbles 'n Bits, 9Lives, Natural Balance, Pup-Peroni, Gravy Train, Nature's Recipe, Canine Carry Outs, and Milo's Kitchen. Big Heart Pet Brands was acquired by The J.M. Smucker Company in 2015 and no longer operates as an independent entity. All brands are now managed under J.M. Smucker's Pet Foods division.
